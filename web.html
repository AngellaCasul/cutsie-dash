About the Game

Dodge, dash & survive!

Cutsie-Dash is an adorable endless runner where you play as a squishy pink creature hopping between lanes to dodge incoming blue cube enemies. But now, it's not just about survival but you can also collect coins scattered across the lanes to boost your score and challenge yourself even more. The longer you survive and the more coins you grab, the higher your score So...Can you beat your personal best?

1
Pick your lane — move up or down between three lanes using arrow keys or WASD.
2
Dodge the cubes — grumpy blue obstacles come flying from the right. Switch lanes to avoid them!
3
Collect coins — grab as many coins as you can while staying alive to maximize your score.
4
Watch for warnings when the game speeds up, a countdown appears before the screen flips.
5
Adapt fast— controls and directions reverse after the flip, so stay focused!
6
Beat your best— survive as long as possible. Each level gets faster and trickier. Press P or ESC to pause.
